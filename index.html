About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Stephanie Burlinchon, and I am a self-taught JavaScript developer. I come from a background in Mathematics, where I honed my analytical and problem-solving skills. Graduating from Queen Mary University of London in 2011.

After spending 10 years in administration, from Personal Assistant to Office Manager, I decided to pursue a career change and dive into the world of technology. I taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and have since been developing my skills as a front-end developer.

What I love about coding is the endless potential for problem-solving and the satisfaction that comes with creating something from scratch. I enjoy working on challenging projects and finding creative solutions to complex problems.

My passion for technology has driven me to constantly learn and improve my skills. I am dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in the field, and I am always eager to take on new challenges.

Please feel free to browse my portfolio and see some of my recent work. If you have any questions or would like to discuss potential projects, please don't hesitate to get in touch. Thank you for visiting my website!
